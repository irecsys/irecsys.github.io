About the City of Chicago

Recommender Systems

Recommender systems typically produce a list of recommendations tailored to user preferences. They are utilized in a variety of areas including e-commerce, educations, movies, music, news, books, research articles, search queries, social tags, and products in general. Welcome to join the research on personalization and recommender systems.

Research

Research Interests

His research interests lie in Data Science, FinTech, Personalizations, Recommender Systems, Human-Centric Computing, Technology-Enhanced Learning, and so forth. It could be related to the following topics:

  • General Areas: Data Science, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence
  • Web & Learning Intelligence: Personalization, Recommender Systems, Learning Analytics, etc.
  • HCI and Users: User Modeling, Human-Centric Computing, Behavior Analysis, etc.
  • FinTech: AI in Finance, portfolio optimization, financial recommender systems, etc.
