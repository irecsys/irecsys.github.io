About the City of Chicago

Chicago, on Lake Michigan in Illinois, is well-known as "Windy City", and it is among the largest cities in the U.S. Famed for its bold architecture. Chicago is an international hub for finance, commerce, industry, technology, telecommunications, and transportation.

Illinois Institute of Technology

Illinois Institute of Technology is a private, technology-focused, research university offering undergraduate and graduate degrees in engineering, science, architecture, design, psychology, humanities, applied technology, business, and law. Recommender Systems

Recommender systems typically produce a list of recommendations tailored to user preferences. They are utilized in a variety of areas including e-commerce, educations, movies, music, news, books, research articles, search queries, social tags, and products in general. Welcome to join the research on personalization and recommender systems.
